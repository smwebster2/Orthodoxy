Welcome to Orthodox Christianity

Orthodox Christianity is one of the three main branches of Christianity, tracing its roots back to the early Church established by the Apostles. With a rich tradition of theology, liturgy, and community life, Orthodoxy continues to thrive in many parts of the world today.
